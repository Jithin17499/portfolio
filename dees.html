Dee's Fresh Meat is a startup that specializes in providing high-quality, ethically sourced, fresh-cut meats to restaurants, grocery stores, and butcher shops. The objective of this branding project is to create a unified visual identity that communicates the quality, freshness, and sustainability of the Dee's Fresh Meat product line. The branding should convey a sense of trustworthiness and should be attractive to consumers and businesses alike. The design should also be versatile, allowing for variations to be used across various types of media and marketing materials.

    •

    • Dee's Fresh Meat is a startup business providing high quality, sustainably sourced meats. The company sources their meats from small-scale family farms that practice sustainable farming practices and they are committed to using only the freshest, healthiest ingredients. Their meats are also free from hormones, antibiotics, and other additives, making them a healthier option for consumers. The company offers a variety of products, including beef, pork, poultry, and seafood, as well as sides and accompaniments such as kabobs and marinades. Customers can order online or visit their retail store to select the cuts of meat they need. Dee's Fresh Meat is committed to providing customers with the best quality, freshest meats available, while also supporting sustainable farming practices.
