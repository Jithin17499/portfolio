The objective of this project is to create a branding strategy for Streamline Educational App, a mobile learning platform for students of all ages. The goal of the branding strategy should be to create a cohesive, professional, and easily recognizable identity that resonates with students and instructors to build trust and loyalty. The branding strategy should be comprehensive, covering the logo design, graphic design, and messaging of the app. The overall goal is to create a successful brand that is recognizable and appealing to the target audience.

    •

    • Streamline App is a platform that allows teachers to provide home tuitions to their students. With Streamline App, teachers are able to easily manage their tutoring sessions, track student progress and manage payments. The app also provides useful tools such as lesson plans, progress reports, and even an online whiteboard for virtual tutoring. Streamline App makes it easy for teachers to connect with students, manage their tutoring sessions, and track their progress. It is a great way for teachers to offer quality home tuition and provide students with the best learning experience.
